A web-based audio processor that allows you to speed up, slow down, and apply various effects to your music files directly in your browser.

## üéµ Features

- **File Upload**: Drag & drop or click to upload MP3, WAV, and other audio files
- **Speed Control**: Adjust playback speed from 0.25x to 3x
- **Pitch Control**: Modify pitch from -24 to +24 semitones
- **Volume Control**: Adjust volume from 0% to 200%
- **Quick Effects**:
  - Slowed & Reverb
  - Sped Up
  - Nightcore
  - Daycore
  - Echo
  - Deep Voice
  - Chipmunk
- **Real-time Processing**: Apply effects instantly while playing
- **Multi-language Support**: English and Arabic
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and mobile
- **Visual Effects**: Interactive 3D particle visualizer

## üöÄ How to Use

1. **Upload Files**: Drag and drop audio files or click "Choose Files"
2. **Select Track**: Click on any uploaded file to load it
3. **Apply Effects**:
   - Use sliders for speed, pitch, and volume
   - Click quick effect buttons for preset combinations
4. **Process & Download**: Click "Process Audio" then "Download Result"
5. **Switch Language**: Click the globe icon for Arabic/English
